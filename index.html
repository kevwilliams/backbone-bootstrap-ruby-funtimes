Q. Kevvos? Seriously? What the %$&^ is this supposed to be?

A. Well... its a rudimentary outfit generator where you can choose which "slots" to put items in. After designing your outfit you hit Download Image and receive an image with the pictures in the appropriate spots, and of course my face.

Uhh.. okay mate.. thats uhh kinda cool I guess.. Hows it work?

The products are contained in a JSON object that is created by scraping the links off a few zappos.com pages using Ruby and Mechanize.

When the page loads Backbone does a quick AJAX request to get the Hats and fills the view and calls Render which displays the products in the list

The user can click a product and that item will be selected into the appropriate "Slot" and the price will increment.
