Backbone

I read something out about Backbone on the Zappos Insider Technology page, and immediately went to work figuring it out. I've been tweaking in Angular and Node for awhile now which might have made understanding it a bit easier. I also found the syntax of this library similar to Prototype, which I am very comfortable in. Something I will certainly continue to use, so lightweight and powerful [Value #8 ].
